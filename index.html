About Us

Uni-Protect CCTV Services provides affordable, reliable, and high-quality security solutions designed to help protect homes, small offices, and business establishments. We are committed to delivering dependable surveillance systems that ensure safety, peace of mind, and continuous monitoring for our clients. We specialize in the installation, configuration, troubleshooting, and maintenance of CCTV systems, ensuring that every setup is properly optimized for clear monitoring and long-term performance. Whether it is a new installation or an upgrade of an existing system, we provide tailored solutions based on the specific needs of each client. Our goal is to make security accessible and effective by offering cost-efficient services without compromising quality. We also assist clients in choosing the right CCTV equipment and positioning to maximize coverage and visibility. At Uni-Protect CCTV Services, we value trust, reliability, and customer satisfaction—ensuring that every project is completed with professionalism and attention to detail.
